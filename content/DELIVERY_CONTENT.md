## DELIVERY: NWL

### Delivery Overview

NWL's delivery scope comprises a 2 MLD MABR wastewater treatment plant with civil infrastructure, delivered as a single turnkey EPC contract by Colubris Water Solutions (Netherlands). The delivery model is designed to place technology risk entirely with the EPC contractor through performance guarantees and warranties.

### Key Delivery Milestones

- **M0**: Financial close and EPC contract execution
- **M0–M18**: Construction phase — Colubris holds technology risk, WSP as owners engineer
- **M18**: Commercial Operations Date (COD) — plant handed over with performance certificates
- **M18–M24**: Grace period — operational ramp-up, DSRA funded

## DELIVERY: LanRED

### Delivery Overview

LanRED's delivery depends on the selected scenario. Under **Greenfield**, LanRED procures new-build solar PV (2.4 MWp) and BESS (1.5 MWh) at Lanseria via a single EPC contract. Under **Brownfield+**, LanRED acquires an operational 5-site portfolio (2.17 MWp, 4 MWh) with Day 1 PPA revenue, followed by Phase 2 Lanseria expansion from retained earnings. LanRED is independently underwritten or has the provision of a cross-currency swap.

## DELIVERY: Timberworx

### Delivery Overview

Timberworx delivery comprises two asset categories:

1. **Centre of Excellence (CoE)** — €1.66M training, demonstration, and light manufacturing facility delivered by BG&E (SYSTRA parent, Australia). Content split: 60% Finland, 30% France, 10% South Africa.

2. **Panel Equipment** — €200k Optimat panel-making machines from the Netherlands (100% Dutch content, Atradius-covered). Enables scale-up from Phase 1 (1 house/week) to Phase 2 (3 houses/week).

### JV Delivery Structure

Timberworx operates within a three-way partnership:
- **BG&E (SYSTRA)** — EPC contractor for CoE, design and engineering, European timber sourcing
- **Greenblock.co.za** — On-site erect & finish contractor, NHBRC registered, SANS compliance
- **Finnish Raw Material JV** — Timber supply from Finnish sawmills (Metsä, UPM, Stora Enso)

## DELIVERY: SCLCA

### Project Integrator

Colubris Water Solutions (Netherlands) serves as the project integrator and wrapper across all three delivery scopes, ensuring consistent quality standards, procurement coordination, and export credit compliance across the portfolio.

### Delivery Scopes

The total project comprises three delivery scopes corresponding to the three operating companies:
- **NWL**: Turnkey MABR plant — single EPC (Colubris)
- **LanRED**: Solar + BESS — single EPC (pending selection)
- **Timberworx**: CoE + Panel Equipment — single EPC (BG&E/DG&E)

### ECA Content Compliance

All delivery scopes are structured to comply with OECD 2021 export credit guidelines:
- **Local Content Cap**: 50% of ECV for Category 2 countries (South Africa)
- **Dutch Content**: Minimum 20% required for Atradius coverage
- **Finnish Content**: Minimum 27-33% for Finnvera consideration (Phase 3)
