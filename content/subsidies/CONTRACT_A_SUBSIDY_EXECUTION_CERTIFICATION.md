## 1. Purpose and Roles

1.1 The purpose of this Agreement is to appoint the Subsidy Agent to support the Exporter/Vendor in preparing, submitting and administering a subsidy application (the "**Subsidy**") and to set out the roles of the Parties in this process.

1.2 The Exporter/Vendor is the applicant and recipient of the Subsidy.

1.3 The Subsidy Agent prepares documents, coordinates submissions and manages reporting.

1.4 The Orchestrator coordinates the wider project and relies on factual certification under this Agreement but does not apply for or receive Subsidy funds.

---

## 2. Definitions and Interpretation

2.1 "**Target Subsidy Amount**" means the indicative subsidy amount specified in Schedule 1.

2.2 "**Award Certificate**" means the certificate in the form of Annex 1 signed by the Exporter/Vendor and Subsidy Agent confirming Subsidy award/disbursement amounts and the payment schedule.

2.3 "**Adjustment Certificate**" means an Award Certificate reflecting a Subsidy amount lower than the Target Subsidy Amount.

2.4 "**Net Available Amount**" means the Subsidy amount minus the Subsidy Agent Fee.

2.5 Schedules and Annexes form part of this Agreement.

---

## 3. Subsidy Process and Cooperation

3.1 The Subsidy Agent shall carry out all reasonable steps to prepare, submit and administer the subsidy application.

3.2 The Exporter/Vendor shall provide necessary information and cooperate in good faith.

3.3 Each Party shall promptly respond to enquiries from the subsidy authority (the "**Authority**").

---

## 4. Representations and Warranties of the Exporter/Vendor

4.1 The Exporter/Vendor represents and warrants that:

(a) all information, documents and statements provided are true, complete and not misleading;

(b) it satisfies all eligibility requirements of the relevant subsidy programme;

(c) no double funding exists or will be sought for the same eligible costs;

(d) it is not subject to investigation, insolvency proceedings or regulatory action that may affect the subsidy;

(e) it shall comply with all applicable Dutch, EU and local laws;

(f) it shall immediately notify the other Parties of any change affecting eligibility.

---

## 5. Target Subsidy Amount and Subsidy Agent Fee

5.1 The Target Subsidy Amount is as set out in Schedule 1.

5.2 The Subsidy Agent Fee equals [●] percent of the Target Subsidy Amount and is expressed as a fixed euro amount.

5.3 The Subsidy Agent Fee becomes payable only upon issuance of an Award Certificate.

5.4 If the Subsidy awarded is lower than the Target Subsidy Amount, the Subsidy Agent Fee is adjusted proportionally through an Adjustment Certificate.

---

## 6. Award Certificate

6.1 Upon receipt of a Subsidy award or disbursement, the Exporter/Vendor shall prepare an Award Certificate in the form of Annex 1.

6.2 The Award Certificate shall state (i) the gross Subsidy amount awarded/disbursed; (ii) the payment schedule; and (iii) the Net Available Amount.

6.3 The Exporter/Vendor and the Subsidy Agent shall sign the Award Certificate confirming that its contents correspond to the Authority's documentation.

6.4 The Subsidy Agent's signature constitutes factual verification only and does not guarantee future eligibility or compliance.

---

## 7. Adjustment Certificate

7.1 Where the Subsidy awarded or disbursed is lower than the Target Subsidy Amount, the Exporter/Vendor may issue an Adjustment Certificate.

7.2 The Adjustment Certificate shall be in the same form as the Award Certificate and shall adjust the Subsidy Agent Fee and Net Available Amount proportionally.

7.3 Issuing an Adjustment Certificate is a right, not an obligation, of the Exporter/Vendor.

---

## 8. Effect of Certificates

8.1 A duly signed Award or Adjustment Certificate constitutes conclusive evidence of the amounts and schedules stated therein, save for manifest error.

8.2 The amounts in a Certificate take effect automatically and require no further approvals.

8.3 Delay in issuing an Adjustment Certificate does not invalidate an existing Award Certificate.

---

## 9. Business Development Activation Event and Standstill

9.1 A Business Development Activation Event occurs upon the Authority's written decision awarding the Subsidy to the Exporter/Vendor.

9.2 Until occurrence of the Activation Event, execution of business development budget obligations is subject to a standstill.

9.3 Upon occurrence of the Activation Event, the standstill lapses automatically without further notice.

---

## 10. Payment of the Subsidy Agent Fee

10.1 The Subsidy Agent Fee shall be paid in accordance with the amounts and schedule stated in the applicable Certificate.

10.2 Payments shall be made within ten (10) Business Days following each payment date in the Certificate.

10.3 Where the Subsidy is paid in instalments, the Subsidy Agent Fee shall be paid proportionally.

---

## 11. Records and Information

11.1 The Exporter/Vendor shall retain all official subsidy documentation for at least five (5) years after final disbursement and provide copies upon request.

11.2 The Parties shall treat all information exchanged under this Agreement as confidential except as required by law or the Authority.

---

## 12. No Guarantee of Award

NexusNovus Capital B.V. and the Subsidy Agent do not guarantee approval, timing, amount or continued eligibility of any subsidy. All authority decisions remain solely within the discretion of the relevant governmental body.

---

## 13. Clawback and Recovery Risk Allocation

If the subsidy is reduced, withdrawn, suspended or recovered due to acts or omissions of the Exporter/Vendor, the Subsidy Agent Fee shall remain earned, due and non-refundable.

---

## 14. Priority of Subsidy Agent Fee

The Subsidy Agent Fee shall have priority over distribution of the Net Available Amount and shall be paid first from each subsidy tranche received.

---

15.1 The Exporter/Vendor shall indemnify and hold harmless NexusNovus Capital B.V. and the Subsidy Agent from and against any and all losses, damages, penalties, fines, clawbacks, costs and expenses (including legal fees) arising out of or in connection with:

(b) breach of eligibility conditions;

(c) clawback, recovery or suspension of the subsidy caused by acts or omissions of the Exporter;

(d) regulatory non-compliance attributable to the Exporter;

(e) third-party claims arising from project implementation.

## 16. Limitation of Liability

16.1 Except in cases of wilful misconduct or fraud, the aggregate liability of NexusNovus Capital B.V. and the Subsidy Agent shall be limited to the amount of the Subsidy Agent Fee actually received.

16.2 Neither NexusNovus nor the Subsidy Agent shall be liable for indirect, consequential, special or punitive damages, including loss of profit or opportunity. No liability shall arise from decisions, delays or actions of any subsidy authority.

---

## 17. Compliance with Laws and Anti-Corruption

Each Party shall comply with applicable Dutch, EU and international anti-bribery, anti-corruption and anti-money laundering laws. The Exporter warrants that no facilitation payments or improper advantages shall be offered in connection with the subsidy application.

---

## 18. Data Protection (GDPR)

The Parties shall comply with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR). Personal data shall be processed only for purposes of this Agreement and in accordance with applicable Dutch data protection laws.

---

## 19. Late Payment and Recovery Costs

Late payments shall accrue interest at 12% per annum above the European Central Bank base rate. All recovery costs, including legal fees, shall be borne by the defaulting Party. No right of set-off shall apply against the Subsidy Agent Fee.

---

## 20. Term and Termination

20.1 This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until completion of the Subsidy process or termination under this clause.

20.2 A Party may terminate this Agreement if another Party materially breaches it and fails to remedy the breach within twenty-five (25) Business Days after written notice.

20.3 Termination does not affect prior rights or the validity of any Certificate.

---

## 21. Insolvency and Regulatory Termination

This Agreement may be terminated immediately upon insolvency, bankruptcy, suspension of payments or regulatory prohibition affecting a Party. Termination shall not affect accrued rights or fees earned.

---

22.2 Performance of affected obligations shall be suspended during the force majeure event.
